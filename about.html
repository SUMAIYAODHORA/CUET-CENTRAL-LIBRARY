Welcome to CUET Central Library

❒ The central library, CUET is one of the most essential organs of Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology ( CUET ). It plays a core activity as part of centre of excellence serving the time worthy library services in engineering education in Bangladesh.

The central library is derived from its parent organization which was established in 1968 taking a name as Chittagong Engineering College. Later, proving its demand as an advance teaching capability in engineering sector, it achieved its new identification as Bangladesh Institute of Technology ( BIT ) in 1986. Finally, it debuts with its all the achievements in the name of Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology ( CUET ) in 2003.
At the time of college era the library took its journey with a least number books among the students of individual departments and college teachers only. When it turned into BIT library it expanded its service up to issue return along with rental service within the three departments named Civil Engg. Electrical Engg. And Mechanical Engg.in the session based students.

Apart from this, it has built its institutional repository with Dspace in where all the digital contents produced by CUET scholars and researchers are preserved here to be used as reference in future research activities.

Meanwhile, the library is being arranged to be designated with a new addition of plagiarism checker software TURNITIN which will ensure the retention and improvement of world renounce of the institution providing an authentic and invention based research and education in engineering sector also.

In the upcoming services, the central library, CUET is getting its preparation to reach the destination of RFID based automated library system along with self issue return of books using drop boxes.

The central Library belongs a high powered committee to takes a necessary advice to develop itself time to time, which is consisted of 7 members in where the honorable vice-chancellor takes chair as the president and 5 faculty deans take chair as the members and Librarian takes chair as member secretary of the committee.

 The central library, CUET contains a large number of collection designed with national & international published books, hard copy journals, online journals and e-books, CD, DVD, thesis paper, dissertation, proceeding and so on. At present it belongs more than 52 thousand hard copy books, 1429 hardcopy journals, more than 2200 online journals and e-books, 890 CDs and DVDs on different collections.

The central library provides services not only in the engineering education but also in country's socio economic, history and cultural development taking a special segment in the name of Bangabandhu, Liberation war & Independence of Bangladesh corner which contains a vast number books, rare videos, films and photos regarding our national great leader Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman and liberation war of Bangladesh.

The central Library, CUET operates its services from two individual buildings placed as general section in old building and reference section, Bangabandhu and Liberation War & Independence of Bangladesh corner in new building. To operate the overall library services the new building belongs Librarians and Deputy Librarians office in new building and to operate the general section properly in old building, it belongs the Assistant Librarians Office at old building also.

The central library is kept functional and remained open from 9 A.M to 8 P.M. The general section is closed at 5 P.M. and reference section remains open under its going on services up to 8 P.M. The schedule is activated only in working days excluding all the weekly and govt. holidays declared by CUET authority.


❒ The Central Library has rich collection in the field of Science, Engineering and Technology. It is an open library system to students of CUET, which provides rich collection of books, journals, newsletter, thesis works and CDs. At present the library has more than 50,000 books, 9,475 e-books and 4,51,511 e-journals. The sitting capacity of Central Library is more than 250.

Borrowing Policy

Patrons in the following categories may borrow items from KUET CentralLibrary.

❒ Undergraduate first & second year students of the CUET shall be entitled to borrow 2 (two) volumes (books of General stack) at a time and third & fourth year students of the CUET shall be entitled to borrow 3 (three) volumes (books of General stack) at a time for a period of one month.

❒ Post graduate students in the Masters level of the University shall be entitled to borrow 5 (five) volumes (books of General Stack) at a time for a period of one month.

❒ Research Scholars/ Ph. D. students may be issued a maximum of 10(ten) books and one bound journal at a time for six months.

❒ A Research Assistant will enjoy facilities like an undergraduate student after getting recommendation from the project supervisor.

Returns

❒ Members must return their borrowed items within on or before the due date.

Renewal

❒ A student can renew borrowed book(s) 05 times through online within 30 days.

Recall

❒ Borrower can recall for currently loaned items in urgent need by contacting circulation desk.

❒ CUET Central Library reserves the right to recall borrowed materials before the due date.

Overdue

❒ Item(s) will be considered as overdue if any user fails to return or renew the item(s)within the due date range.

❒ Borrower will not be able to borrow if he/she has overdue.

❒ Borrowing privileges will be suspended until the item(s)is/are returned and the penalty for non-returned item(s)is/are paid.

❒ Notices will be served through e-mail to student/staff e-mail accounts.

❒ Borrowers are responsible for checking that e-mail account.

Overdue Fines

❒ Tk. 1.00 (One) only per calendar day for each Book item after the due date.

Lost Item

❒ In case a volume or any other library materials is lost or damaged while on loan, the borrower shall be held responsible and shall report it immediately.

❒ The borrower will be solely liable for the full replacement of cost and accumulated fines for the item(s) borrowed.

❒ He/she shall be required to replace the volume within one month from the date of report of missing otherwise pay double amount of the current price of this volume or library materials as determined by the Librarian.

General Rules

❒Guidlines about entering into the reference section of Library:

❒ Before entering the library the readers shall handover their personal books, sticks, umbrellas, parcels, opened or closed bags, attaches, portfolios, brief cases, newspapers etc. to the Library Attendant at the checking counter.

❒ Deposited materials will have to be collected from the checking counter at least before 15 minutes of the closing of the Library on the same day.

❒ No one will be permitted to enter the library with fire producing materials.

❒ Books issued for study in the respective reading room shall not be removed elsewhere without specific written permission.

❒ Students will be allowed to the Stack on production of Identity Cards. Teachers, officers and research scholars are also allowed to the stack by introducing themselves.

❒ Reading room facilities shall be allowed to students.

❒ If any reader walks out of the Library with any Library property, the Librarian will report to the Vice-Chancellor for necessary action

❒ Loud voice, eating, drinking, smoking in the Library are prohibited.

❒ All library users are required to maintain a quiet and pleasant atmosphere in the library and cooperate with library administration abiding by Library rules. Library privileges may be denied to persons who behave otherwise.

❒ About rare Collections:

❒ Encyclopedias, dictionaries and other reference materials shall not, as a rule, be loaned out of the library but these may be consulted in the library.

❒ Reserved books shall not be lent out.

❒ Borrowers and readers are expected to check defects in any books issued to them and immediately call the attention of the library official on duty. Borrower shall be held responsible for any damage detected at the time of return and he shall be required to make good the loss.

❒ In case a volume or any other library material is mutilated or disfigured while on loan, the borrower shall be held responsible and in such cases, he/she shall be required to make good the loss and may be debarred from borrowing any further books from the Library.

❒ No one shall be entitled to borrow books in more than one capacity.

❒ The Librarian may refuse to lend out books except to the borrower in person.

❒ Borrowing privileges shall be withdrawn from members until overdue volumes are returned and fines paid by them.

❒ For the publishing of the final result of any academic program the students must submit their library clearance to the controller of Examination.

❒ Audio-visual support of the library will be provided to the users deemed necessary to the Librarian or his authorized officer.

Sections

Acquisition Section:

❒ This section performs the major activity of collection development/ procurement of new library materials (books, journals, periodicals etc.) based on the recommendations made by the Head of the concerned departments. This task is performed by Library Administration section.

Processing Section:

❒This section performs the major professional activities like Classification, and Cataloguing. The Library is using International Standard Classification DDC 21 st edition Scheme for classification, AACR II for cataloguing, Library of Congress Subject Headings for determination of Subject Heading. For Library Automation, books are catalogued according to MARC 21 by using Koha-ILS software.

Circulation Section:

❒ In circulation section, the users of the library can borrow the reading materials by using their Machine Readable Library Card. Under graduate students can borrow three (03) books, M. Sc, M. Phil and Ph. D students (regular) can borrow five (05) books at a time for one month. A student can also renew borrowed book(s) 05 times through online within 30 days. Teachers can borrow fifteen (15) books at a time for one semester. Officers can borrow two (02) books at a time for a period of one month. Class III employees can borrow one (01) book at a time only for two weeks.

Reference Section:

❒ Dictionaries, encyclopedias, and rare books are generally kept in the reference section. This section is opened from 09.00 am to 5.00 pm. (Sunday to Thursday). Nobody can borrow any book from this section. But sometimes in special cases, librarian can give the special permission to issue these books. Users can use these books any time according to their needs.

Journal Section:

❒ Journal section is one of the important sections for research purposes. As a research oriented university, central library collects huge journals on various fields. Library also subscribes journals on specific recommendations by the Head of the concerned departments. This section is opened from 09.00 AM to 5.00 PM. (Sunday to Thursday). This section also kept various proceedings, thesis on M. Sc. M. Phil and Ph. D. and dissertations.

Newspaper Section:

❒ CUET Central Library has a large Newspaper Section, which kept 10 national and local daily newspapers for the users. This section is opened from 10.00 AM to 8.00 PM. (Sunday to Thursday).

Career Development Corner

❒ CUET Central Library has a Career Development Corner, which provide students many national and international books(BCS, IELTS, GRE etc.) for the development of their career. This section is opened from 10.00 AM to 8.00 PM. (Sunday to Thursday) and also opened from 09.00 AM to 01.00 PM (Saturday).

Services

Digital Library Access Center Service:

❒ The recent addition of central library is the Digital Library Access Center. It has some Desktop Computers with high speed internet connectivity for the library users. From this Center, users can browse, OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue) search and download e-resources (e-journals, e-books etc.). In a word, Digital Library Access Center can meet the demand of e-resources to the users of Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology in an electronic way.

Reading Room Service:

❒There is a large modern reading room in the Central Library where the students can study in a very good environment. In this room they can read all the books of the library. At present more than 100 library users can seat at a time for reading purposes. In this reading room, there is a Special Corner with well decorated which name is Bangabandhu, Muktijuddha o Bangladesh . In this corner, a huge collection on Biography of Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, Bangladesh Liberation War, History of Bangladesh and Bengali Literature.

Photocopy Services:

❒ Every user can photocopy their necessary materials from photocopy section. For photocopying, they have to pay by using a coupon which can get Departmental Store, CUET.

E- Resource Services:

❒ Central Library of Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology has one of the best e-resource centers among the Public Universities in Bangladesh. All Faculty Members, Officers, Staffs and Students of central library can get access to these e-journals and e-books without password from inside the campus through this website.

Location of Library

❒ Central Library is located on the Second and Third floors of the A Block at the New Academic Complex, which is situated near the sculpture 'Durbar Bangla'.
